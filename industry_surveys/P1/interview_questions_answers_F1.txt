Fault Related Questions:

Q: What's the appearance of the fault?
A: Messages are displayed in wrong order.

Q: How to reproduce the fault?
A: When user submit more than one messages in quick manner, the issue will happen.

Q: How many microservices involved in this case?
A: 3.

Q: How much time spent to locate the fault?
A: 10 days.

Q: What's the root cause of the fault?
A: Asynchronous message delivery doesn't have sequence control.

Q: Describe the detail process of the fault localication?
A: First check the message sending logic,
   Second check the code change, sync --> async
   Third check the sequence control, locate the issue.
